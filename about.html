Gift-givers want a gift that feels memorable and hand-crafted

But most newly-weds want cash, which can feel impersonal and uncreative.

The next version of Hautewheels will let you:

1. Pick a Hotwheels car for $10

2. Send any amount of cash or crypto of your choice.

3. Allow the recipient to convert the gift to any currency of their choice (USD, BTC, ETH).
